Our Story

Founded in the spring of 2022, in the heart of Orlando, our team was inspired by the start of spring. We began looking for ways to rediscover the land all around us. We figured what better way to start then the local trails around us and read the reviews to get a clear understanding of what we were going to embark on. From this time forth "That Good Dirt " was born and the rest is history.

Our Mission

Our love for nature and helping our community drove us to making a user friendly platform that ultimately helps the way people get outside and see where their nearest, best and future planned trails.
